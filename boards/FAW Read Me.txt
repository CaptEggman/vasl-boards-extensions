FAW J1a and J1b Vasl Maps Version 1.0 released June 2011. Drawn by Pete Roberts

These are hand-drawn versions of Critical Hit's Finland at War J1a and J1b maps, there may be slight differences in LOS and terrain depictions from the original

Thanks to Ray Tapio at Critical Hit for permission to VASLize the maps.

The extension adds the counters from Critical Hit's Finland at War to the Finnish order of battle.
 
A new 5-5-8 squad, 2-4-8 HS and a TH Hero can be found under the existing Squad, HS and SMC tabs.

Three additional MGs can be found under the SW tab.

A new FAW tab is where to find an additional 21 vehicles and  5 Guns.
