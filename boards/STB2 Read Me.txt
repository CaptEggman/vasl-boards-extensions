STB2 Vasl Map Version 1.0 released Sept 2011. Drawn by Pete Roberts

This is a hand-drawn version of Critical Hit's Scotland The Brave II map, there may be slight differences in LOS and terrain depictions from the original

STB2 is designed to join up with STB1

To join the maps just load STB2, add a row and select STB1.
