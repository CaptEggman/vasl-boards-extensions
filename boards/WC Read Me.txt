Witches Cauldron Vasl Maps WC1 and WC2 Version 1.0 released 14/2/11. Drawn by Pete Roberts 

These are a hand-drawn versions of Critical Hit's Witches Cauldron maps WC1 and WC2, there may be slight differences in LOS and terrain depictions from the original.

The maps can be cropped in the normal manner in Vasl.  For the complete map load WC1 with WC2 as an added row.

Thanks to Ray Tapio at Critical Hit for permission to VASLize these maps.
